Carbon Footprint Tracker

Developed by Aarav Wattal

Carbon Footprint Tracker is the best way to identify your carbon emissions and reduce them. Every day, the app refreshes with new challenges to help the environment!

The app includes many features, including the following:

- Keep track of your daily carbon emissions over time

- View a breakdown of your carbon footprint

- Acquire points from completing challenges

- Learn cool facts about our environment and global warming

The app was created with Swift and Firebase, and will continue to be updated with new content like leaderboards and graphs to represent user carbon emissions over time.
